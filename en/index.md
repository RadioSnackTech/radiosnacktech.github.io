## Membership

Radio Snack is a membership-based community space. Interested in joining our community? [Become a member!](/membership)

## Workshops

We host workshops and learning sessions for all kinds of creative DIY media,
from technology to textiles. Check out [Upcoming Events](/events) to see what we've
got. We aim to have low-cost, low barrier workshops for all kinds of people.
Want to host a workshop? [Pitch one here](https://forms.gle/xjudVufGqU6ZmfG59)

## Meetups

Radio Snack hosts meet ups, work groups and private events for arts, DIY,
games and other creative groups around Montreal. We have projection equipment
and seating, so if you want to host a meetup, let us know!

## Residencies

Got an idea, or an idea for an idea? Radio Snack Artists In Residence get
access to our full space and community. Get in touch, or have a look out for
our next call for proposals! No specific ideas required.
